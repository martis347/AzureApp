Hello There!

The purpose of this application is to advance the process of ordering lunch.

Since this application is directly editing google spreadsheets, there are some limitations, so please keep in mind:

  • Your name must be consistent between all sheets, that is important!
  • The software has not been fully tested and might contain bugs, so please double check your order in google sheets
  • Your are allowed to edit until 11 AM of current day. (But still keep in mind at what times you're actually allowed to edit) You may also fill further days of current week.
  • Your Document is edited on behalf of you, so it reflects in history of Google spreadsheets. By saying that, I mean don't mess with other peoples names.
  • You're free to leave some feedback about what you like and dislike. You can do that by clicking button at the top right corner.
